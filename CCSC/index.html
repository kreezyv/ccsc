INITIATING THE FIRST FLIGHT: Foreshadowing Leadership and Service Beyond

Duty Eagle eyed, the guiding lights of Cagayan State University Carig Campus are making their way towards a different level of service. Vowed to take their duties beyond the optimum, the Carig Campus Student Council Officers are ready to take on new challenges and responsibilities with a mission to serve. Behold, the CCSC Officers, paving their way to conquer heights as they take flight to serve the whole red eagle family. This is a new age of accomplisments and victorius beginnings and endings. With this again, this is the Carig Campus Student Council initiating the first flight as this academic year begins with leadership and service beyond duty. Padayon, CCSC!
